April 5, 2024

Photos of the week!

Leon is now seven weeks old and about as heavy as a black swan, so let's move on to some post hoc analysis of our favorite little world-altering event.

Leon's favorite new activity is unclenching his fists. It's nice to have a less intimidating baby. We even felt confident enough to have some friends over for dinner. The logic of infancy is opaque and remorseless but, typically, sound.
